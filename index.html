THE ROAD AHEAD

Ride Routes

We ride across Bangalore, exploring scenic routes and creating unforgettable riding experiences. There’s no fixed schedule—reach out to join our next ride and stay connected with our community for the latest updates, destinations, and adventures.

Buddies Unleashed is a motorcycle riding community for people who value safe group riding, route discipline, and continuous learning on the road. We have riders from across India, united by a passion for exploring new routes and building a strong riding culture. Every ride is planned with careful consideration of weather conditions, road safety, rider comfort, and established riding protocols. We believe every journey is a team effort, where experienced riders support newer members in developing confidence in lane positioning, braking techniques, formation riding, and effective communication on the road.

DISCIPLINE ON THE ROAD

Rules & Regulations

Safety Gear is Mandatory

Helmets are non-negotiable for both rider and pillion. Riding jackets, gloves, and proper shoes are strongly advised on all long rides.

Valid Documents

Every rider must carry a valid driving license, RC, insurance, and pollution certificate at all times during the expedition.

Lane Discipline

No reckless overtaking, zigzag riding, or speeding. We ride in formation and respect the pace set by the lead rider.

Brotherhood First

Respect all members. In case of a breakdown or emergency, the clan stays together. No rider is left behind.
